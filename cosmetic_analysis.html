Key Insights

✔ Products containing Hyaluronic Acid had the highest average ratings.

✔ Vitamin C-based serums were more expensive but had better long-term customer satisfaction.

✔ Retinol products showed mixed reviews, with higher sensitivity concerns.

✔ Sunscreens with mineral filters were preferred over chemical filters by 68% of customers.
